REFLECTING BRIDGE



In frame is a beautiful painting of a bridge.
Here also clear reflection can be seen of the greenery and the bridge in the water.
Here the climate shown in the painting is stormy morning.


Materials Required :


  • 1 Canvas (16*16) inch.

  • paint brush.

  • black,dark green,light green,brick red


    • steps one can follow(if needed) :


  • First take a plain canvas and tape it if you want a border or give a wash to the canvas with black
    on top and bottom leaving the centre white.

  • After that, first sketch the bridge scenery properly and then start painting the
    bridge with brick red paint and let it dry for sometime.

  • After that paint the trees and do the same exact copy on the opposite side too (downwards) with teal green.

  • Then start painting the lower walls of the bridge and leave it to dry, start with giving the brick
    design to the bridge with black paint or any black gel pen.

  • Then shade the lower wall of the bridge too with black paint or any black gel pen.

  • Then with white paint just put line in middle of the grass painted for further reflection look.

  • After all this is done , with a white ink pen or just with white acrylic paint make little wave lines on the bottom
    part to show that its a clear reflection in water.

  • Aftwe all this is done, dry it well before framing the painting.

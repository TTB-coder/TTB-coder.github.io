SUNSHINE IN FOREST



In frame is a painting with a little bit of forest look having trees
on which a lot of sunlight is falling on the some of the trees.
the painting is basically showing the strength of daylight in forest.


Materials Required :


  • 1 Canvas (16*16) inch.

  • paint brush.

  • golden,black,brown


    • steps one can follow(if needed) :


  • First take a plain canvas and tape it if you want a border or give wash to the whole canvas with little golden
    first on the top end.

  • After that first paint trees using little ochre yellow and the a little bigger tree with brown a
    little more big tree with brown.

  • After that from up just give little golden strokes with paint brush to show the sunlight effect (make sure to not take too
    much golden paint on the brush and to use a dry brush.

THE GOLDEN GATE BRIDGE



In frame is The Golden Gate Bridge painting.
This bridge is located in San Francisco , CA, United States.
This painting is basically showing the heavy flow of water and the early morning scenery of the bridge.
This painting is just giving that pleasant weather look from those early morning sky and the flow of water.


Materials Required :


  • 1 Canvas (16*14) inch.

  • paint brush.

  • Light green, brick red,white,dark grey,light blue and yellow acrylic colour's.


    • steps one can follow(if needed) :


  • First take a plain canvas and tape it if you want a border or give wash to the whole canvas with dark blue from top
    being darker and lighter towards the middle and the a little yellow shade in the middle merging with little blue wash.

  • After that sketch the bridge or the whole scenery after the base dries.

  • After that paint the bridge with brick red and let it dry well.

  • After that shade the bridge and paint the road and mountain with dark grey and give the mountaina bit
    shade on top with white paint.

  • After all this, paint the water with a wavy stroke throught out with light blue,light
    grey and let it dry before framing it

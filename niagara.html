NIAGARA FALLS



In frame is a front side view of Niagara falls.
Niagara falls is a very famous falls located under province’s of Ontario,Canada.
The Nigara falls is one of the popular
tourist attractions that is been visited by people of Canada as well as USA.


Materials Required :


  • 1 Canvas (14*16) inch.

  • paint brush.

  • Teal green, dark brown,aqua blue,light blue, white acrylic colour’s.


    • steps one can follow(if needed) :


  • First take a plain canvas and tape it if you want a border or give wash to the top part of canvas with
    light blue (dark at the edge to gradually lighter in the middle) and let it dry.

  • After that sketch the waterfall and trees.

  • After that start withe the painting of the brown stones and all and let it dry .

  • After that paint the trees and then start painting the waterfall with a up to dowanward stroke of
    the brush and continue the same stroke for waterfall and let it dry.

  • After that paint the water below the watrfall and let it dry, then start to take white
    paint and dab above the waterfall as done in the above painting to give
    the effect of the water force when it is falling from waterfall.

  • Dry it well before framing it.
